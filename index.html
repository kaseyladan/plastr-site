About Us

plastr is a family-owned plastering business founded by brothers Andrej and Dejan (DJ). Known online as the @drywallgangsters, they’ve built more than just a following; they’ve built a reputation across South East Queensland for precision plastering, flawless finishes and no shortcuts.

We deliver end-to-end plastering solutions for new builds and renovations, ensuring every stage of your project is handled with care and expertise.
