About Us

Plastr is a family-owned plastering business founded by brothers Andrej and Dejan (DJ). Known online as the @drywallgangsters, they’ve built more than just a following; they’ve built a reputation across South East Queensland for precision plastering, flawless finishes and no shortcuts.

We deliver end-to-end plastering solutions for new builds and renovations, ensuring every stage of your project is handled with care and expertise.

Why Choose Plastr?

  • Family Owned & Operated – Andrej & DJ bring their shared passion, skill and attention to detail into every project.
  • Skilled & Approachable – Professional, straightforward and easy to work with.
  • Every Job Done Right – From walls and ceilings to detailed finishes, every line, joint and curve is completed with pride.
  • Communication You Can Count On – Clear updates, no surprises and honest advice.
  • Tidy from Start to Finish – Sites are left as clean as the finished walls.
